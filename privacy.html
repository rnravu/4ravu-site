Privacy Policy

How 4ravu.com handles information.

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Overview

4ravu.com is the corporate website of 4Ravu L.L.C. ("4Ravu," "we," "us," or "our"). This site is primarily informational, describing our company, products, and services. This policy explains what information is collected through this website and how it is used.

Information We Collect

4ravu.com does not require account registration and does not knowingly collect sensitive personal information through this website. When you visit this site, our hosting infrastructure may automatically collect basic server log information, such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps. This information is used only for security, troubleshooting, and understanding basic site usage.

If you email us directly (for example, at contact@4ravu.com, support@4ravu.com, or privacy@4ravu.com), we will receive whatever information you choose to include in that message.

How We Use Information

Any information collected through this website is used solely to operate, secure, and improve the site, and to respond to inquiries sent to us directly. We do not sell personal information collected through 4ravu.com, and we do not use this site to serve third-party advertising.

Cookies and Tracking

4ravu.com does not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking scripts. Basic server logs may be retained by our hosting infrastructure as described above.

Product-Specific Privacy Policies

This policy applies only to the 4ravu.com corporate website. Individual 4Ravu products, such as SmartRetireCalc, are governed by their own, product-specific privacy policies, which may address additional data practices relevant to that product. Please refer to the privacy policy published on the relevant product's website.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information related to this website, but no method of transmission or storage over the internet is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@4ravu.com.
