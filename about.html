About 4Ravu L.L.C.

A New Jersey software company building practical, privacy-conscious products.

4Ravu L.L.C. is a New Jersey limited liability company that designs, builds, and operates software products focused on solving real, everyday problems. We believe good software should be practical, trustworthy, and respectful of the people who use it.

What We Focus On

Our work centers on a few core areas:

  • Retirement planning tools — helping people understand and plan for their financial future.
  • AI-enabled productivity tools — using modern AI to simplify everyday tasks and workflows.
  • Web applications — clean, responsive, and reliable software delivered through the browser.
  • Cloud-hosted software — products built to run reliably and scale as needed.

Our Approach

We favor practical solutions over unnecessary complexity, and we build with privacy in mind from the start. Whether we're developing our own products or supporting select client projects, we hold the same standard: software that is useful, dependable, and respectful of user data.
